Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth was born 17th June 1987. He was born and raised in Compton, California, a place known for its high crimerate and gang violence. Growing up in Compton wasn't easy for him, he witnessed his first murder at the age of 5 and was a constant victim of racism as a teenager. However he was able to get up and out from that dark place of violence by doing what he loved, making music. Kendrick's music is known for its lyrical depth and exploring concepts such as racism, therapy, sexual abuse and many more. Till date he has released five total major studio albums.
